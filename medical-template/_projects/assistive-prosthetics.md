## Clinical Motivation

Assistive devices need to be affordable and easy to maintain for broad impact.

## Question

Can modular, low-cost prosthetic components restore essential function affordably?

## Approach (high-level)

- Co-design with users, mechanical prototyping, simple performance tests

## Key Results

- Usability metrics and a small pilot demonstrating improved mobility

## What I Learned

Tradeoffs between cost, robustness, and adjustability.

## Connection to Medicine

Framed around rehabilitation outcomes and access.
